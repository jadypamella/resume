Verification policy

A credential is listed here once it is verifiable through one of three channels: a public verification URL on the issuing platform, the original signed certificate (PDF), or a dated screenshot from the issuer's portal. Each entry clearly states the issuer, the date earned, and the verification channel.

  • Live verification preferred. When the issuer provides a public verification URL (Credly, BlueDot, ISACA, etc.), the Verify badge button links straight to it.
  • PDF or screenshot accepted. When the issuer does not publish a live verification page (older certificates, government training programs, internal corporate certifications), the original PDF or a dated screenshot from the issuer's portal is available on request.
  • No expired credentials. Removed within 30 days of expiry, unless the underlying skill remains a clear professional reference and the issuer continues to recognize historical holders.
  • Issuer-graded only. Listed credentials required graded work (exam, assignment, or capstone), not just a paid registration.

Need to confirm a credential not listed here, or request the original PDF? Email hello@jadypamella.com.
